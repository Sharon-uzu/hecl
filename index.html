About Us

Operational Excellence, Uncompromising Safety, and Sustainable Innovation.

Heavy Engineering Construction Limited, is an established, dynamic Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) firm dedicated to serving the evolving needs of the global Oil and Gas Industrial Sector. Founded by an industry veteran with decades of experience in upstream, midstream, and downstream infrastructure, we bridge the gap between complex engineering challenges and high-performance reality that fosters precision engineering, robust fabrication, and strategic project management with an uncompromising safety culture.

We pride ourselves on an agile approach, utilizing modern technologies to deliver turnkey solutions that are as efficient as they are reliable. As we look to the future, Heavy Engineering Construction Ltd is committed to driving growth and innovation while fostering strong partnerships with our clients and stakeholders.
